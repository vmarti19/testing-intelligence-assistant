4x4 Shift in Progress and 4x4 Shifting Instructions Warning Description

The purpose of the 4x4 Shift in Progress Warning is to provide clarifying information to the driver indicating that the drivetrain is in the process of shifting between 4x4 modes (2WD, 4x4 Auto, 4x4 High, 4x4 Low).
